📋 Privacy Policy
1. Data Collection
TVT CHAT sirf zaroor data collect karta hai — email, display name, messages aur call logs.

2. Messages
Aapke messages Firebase servers par securely store hote hain. Hum private conversations access nahi karte.

3. Calls
Voice aur video calls WebRTC se hoti hain — end-to-end. Call content record nahi hoti.

4. Personal Info
Aapka email sirf login ke liye hai. Hum info kisi third party ko share nahi karte.

5. Status
Status 24 ghante baad automatically delete hoti hai.

6. Security
Account Firebase Authentication se secure hai.

7. Support

Send Messages
Tap pencil icon, search by email/phone. Type message and press Send or Enter.
Voice Messages
Tap mic button to start recording. Tap again to send. Cancel to discard.
Voice & Video Calls
Open a chat, tap phone or video icon. Other user gets a notification to accept or decline.
Status Updates
Status tab → My Status → write text → POST. Contacts who saved your number see it for 24h.
Contacts
Add contacts by phone or email. When they join TVT CHAT, they appear in your contacts.
Delete Messages
Long-press any message → Delete for Me, or Delete for Everyone (only your own messages).
Online Status
Green dot = online. Grey = offline. Shown next to name in chat list and on chat header.
Message Ticks
✓✓ grey = delivered. ✓✓ blue = read. Ticks appear when recipient has opened the chat.
